Fewer ignitions. Fewer outages.

For electric utilities, grid operators and managers of linear infrastructure: vegetation along the network is one of the leading ignition vectors. SilvIA directs vegetation management and preventive shutoffs with data, not calendars.

Vegetation, prioritized by real risk.

Optical, radar, LiDAR and thermal data fused over your corridors and rights-of-way, with georeferenced alerts.

Intelligent vegetation management

Detection of encroaching biomass and dead or stressed trees along your lines, so you can send crews exactly where the risk demands it.

Optimised preventive shutoffs (PSPS)

Soil moisture, NDVI and wind at circuit level to fine-tune the timing and geographic scope of each shutoff. Shorter, better-contained outages, and fewer penalties.

Continuous corridor monitoring

Permanent surveillance of the easement strip, with georeferenced alerts whenever a new risk emerges: regrowth, fallen trees or an incipient thermal hotspot.

Investment prioritization

The risk-scoring engine ranks trimming and undergrounding by expected impact, so you invest first where ignition risk falls the most.

API integration with your assets

Alerts and risk scoring connect to your asset management systems (GIS, EAM), so the data lands where your team already works.
