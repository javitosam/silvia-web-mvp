One platform, three ways of working with the forest.

Continuous surveillance, deep on-demand analysis, and an interpretation layer that translates both into plain language.

SilvIA Earth

Interactive maps and continuous alerts across whatever territory you choose to monitor. Sentinel-2 and Landsat optical imagery delivers close to 97% accuracy in cover classification; Sentinel-1 radar cuts through clouds and works at night, enabling 24/7 surveillance; and VIIRS thermal data at 375 m resolution picks up incipient hotspots. Monthly classification into Forest, Non-forest and Degraded forest, supported by the NDVI and NBR indices.

SilvIA Analysis

On-demand analysis over the areas of interest you define. Biomass estimation at 30 m resolution by combining spaceborne LiDAR (GEDI, ICESat-2) with Sentinel and Landsat; multi-decade time series to establish solid baselines; and anomaly detection alongside forest degradation monitoring. Export to GIS formats and CSV, plus automated reports that slot into your own workflow.

SilvIA AI Assistant

Translates every technical detection into plain language, always paired with an explicit confidence level (for example, "85% confidence in the species identification") and actionable recommendations. Never a blind alert: you will know what was detected, how certain we are, and what to do next.

Methodology

Ingestion

Multi-sensor fusion

Optical, radar, LiDAR and thermal data combined into a single, continuous observation record.

Models

What we do, and what we don't.

Signals for better decisions. Not absolute certainties.

What we do

  • Always communicate the level of uncertainty alongside every detection.
  • Provide traceable evidence: which sensor, which date, which model.
  • Validate against ground truth and independent reference datasets.
  • Feed insights from real pilots back into the continuous improvement of our models.

What we don't do

  • We don't promise perfect detection: every model carries a margin of error.
  • We don't hide uncertainty behind a single number or a traffic light.
  • We don't replace field verification or official alert systems.
  • We don't make the decision for you: decision support, not decision authority.
