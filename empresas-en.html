EUDR compliance

The deforestation regulation, without the headaches.

The EUDR already applies: from 30 December 2025 for large operators and from 30 June 2026 for SMEs, with penalties that can reach 4% of annual EU turnover. Proving that your plots of origin are deforestation-free requires geospatial evidence — exactly what the satellite has been recording for years.

You upload your plots

You upload the polygons of the plots of origin, yours or your suppliers', in whatever format you already use.

We check them against the cut-off

Each plot is compared against the reference satellite imagery of 31 December 2020, the cut-off date set by the regulation.

You get the risk level with its evidence

Each plot or batch is classified by deforestation risk level, together with the geospatial evidence that backs it.

You document your due diligence

With that evidence you prepare your Due Diligence Statement with verifiable backing, ready for your records and your audits.

Frequently asked questions

Everything you're wondering about the EUDR and your land.

What is the EUDR and does it affect me?

It's the EU regulation that requires proving that certain products (timber and other commodities such as coffee, cocoa, soy or rubber) do not come from land deforested after 31 December 2020. It affects operators and traders placing them on the EU market.

Do I need technical knowledge to use SilvIA Earth?

No. Alerts and recommendations arrive in plain language, with their explanation and their confidence level. The technical part is on us.

Does it work for a single estate?

Yes. The platform works the same for a family plot as for a corporate portfolio: what changes is the depth of the analysis, not the tool.

Do I have to install anything on my land?

Nothing. SilvIA works exclusively with satellite data: no sensors, no hardware, no installation visits.
