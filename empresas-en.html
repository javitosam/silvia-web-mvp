From a single plot to a corporate portfolio.

The same territory, observed at the depth each decision demands: regulatory compliance, carbon valuation and the health of your standing timber.

EUDR compliance

Every plot is traced against 2020 reference imagery from Landsat and Sentinel, with low / standard / high supplier risk scoring at batch level.

Integration with your systems

The Due Diligence Statement (DDS) is generated automatically and delivered via API to your ERP and to TRACES NT, with no duplicated administrative work.

Biomass and carbon stock

Above-ground biomass inventory at 30 m resolution, combining spaceborne LiDAR (GEDI/ICESat-2) with multispectral signatures, ready for MRV/REDD+ and additionality certification.

Early threat detection

Pests, drought and degradation identified before they become visible to the naked eye: for instance, detecting the pine wood nematode by combining computer vision with multispectral signatures.

A view spanning decades

Multi-decade time series to set the baseline for each plot, backing your carbon and compliance declarations with verifiable evidence.

Exports and reporting

Data and layers ready for your own workflow: GIS export in GeoTIFF, CSV and automated reports for audits, certification bodies and internal teams.

Process

The EUDR workflow, step by step.

The EUDR applies from 30 December 2025 (mid-2026 for SMEs), with penalties of up to 4% of annual EU turnover.

Upload your polygons

You upload the plot polygons for your suppliers, in whatever format you already work with.

Cross-check against 2020 imagery

Each plot is automatically checked against the reference imagery for the deforestation cut-off date of 31 December 2020.

Risk classification

Each supplier is assigned a low, standard or high risk level at batch level, together with the geospatial evidence behind it.

DDS generated and integrated

The Due Diligence Statement (DDS) is generated and sent via API to your ERP and to TRACES NT, ready to file.
