A team between the forest and the data.

We build SilvIA from Leganés (Madrid), bringing together forestry engineering, Earth observation and artificial intelligence, so that whoever manages a territory can decide with the same information we see from the satellite.

Our mission

Born from data, here for the forests.

SilvIA was born out of a frustration we knew first-hand: the people managing land and forests were still relying on scattered, slow tools that were hard to interpret, while the data to do better had been up there for years, orbiting over our heads. So we set out to build the platform we felt was missing: one capable of turning all that Earth observation into clear, timely decisions.

Over time, that initial frustration has grown into the purpose that now drives everything we do: helping to protect Spain from large forest fires. Every season, thousands of hectares, and too many lives, are at stake, and we are convinced that anticipating risk with objective data, knowing where to look, when to act and with what degree of certainty, can make the difference between arriving late and arriving in time.

None of this is meant to replace the people who know the land, let alone the official alert systems. Our job is easier to state than to do: putting the best possible information in the hands of those who decide, so they can act before the damage becomes irreversible.
