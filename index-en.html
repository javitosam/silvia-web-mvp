How it works

Three layers of data, one answer: where the risk is.

A wildfire doesn't come out of nowhere. It needs dry fuel, terrain that favours it and specific weather conditions. SilvIA Earth monitors these and other variables continuously across your whole territory and turns them into a prioritized risk map: which areas are most likely to ignite this week, and with what degree of confidence. It's not a generic traffic light for an entire province: it's operational prioritization calibrated for each ecosystem — which hills to patrol, which firebreaks to clear and which areas to inspect before the next heat wave.

PredictionDays of lead time, not minutes. Risk is anticipated before ignition, while prevention is still possible.
Coverage100% of your territory. The satellite leaves nothing unwatched, day or night, clouds or not.
ConfidenceExplicit confidence. Every signal states its degree of certainty. Never a blind alert.
ActionFrom data to decision. Georeferenced alerts, ready to mobilize teams on the ground.

Prediction, not detection

By the time a system detects the fire, prevention has already failed.

Almost every wildfire technology on the market does the same thing: warn you once something is already burning. Cameras, sensors, thermal satellites… they all look at the present.

SilvIA Earth looks ahead. Our model flags the areas most likely to ignite before any fire exists, so budget goes into preventing wildfires and not only into putting them out. Prediction, not detection: that's the difference between responding and preventing.
