The core of SilvIA

Wildfire risk shows up in the data days before the first flame.

The risk engine crosses fuel dryness, topography and weather to flag where ignition is most likely. And when a thermal hotspot does appear, the satellite confirms it within minutes: by day, by night or through clouds. Every signal arrives with its confidence level made explicit. Traditional detection takes 48 hours on average; forests can't wait that long, and neither does SilvIA.
